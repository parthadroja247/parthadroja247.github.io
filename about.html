End to End Development, What?

I’ve discovered that in the development of every digital product, there is a continuous conflict between the business side, user requirements, and the technical solution. This has led me to re-define my approach to product development:

The 3D’s Discover, Develop, Deliver. Discover is a part of gathering requirements in all terms like compliance, regulations, customers need, end user of the product etc. Develop is a part of implementing product, testing the product. Deliver is a part of releasing the product to the end customers.

In my opinion, the best solutions should: recognize a business opportunity, react according to the market, scale up or down, consider the users, respect the high-end UI/UX industry standards, and ceaselessly optimize for development. I’m always looking beyond the develop part of the process.
